BENGAL

Bengals are known for the pattern or design of their skin. They are cats that are gracious, brave and strong. Bengals are considered unique and special as their appearance look like leopards, tigers and ocelots.

SPHYNX

These kind of cats are known for them having no fur or hair. Although, they are not completely hairless, they still have some slight fur or hair. They are perfect and safe for people who have allergies too. Sphynx cats are smart and curious.

SIAMESE

These kind of cats are known to be long, muscular and tubular. The shape of their head are slighty triangular and they have glossy, fine and short fur or hair. Siamese cats are playful, intelligent and very active.
